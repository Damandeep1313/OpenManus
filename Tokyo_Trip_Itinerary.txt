Day 1: Arrival and Exploration
- Morning: Arrive in Tokyo, check into your hotel.
- Afternoon: Visit Asakusa and the Senso-ji Temple.
- Evening: Dinner at a local izakaya (Japanese pub).

Day 2: Cultural Immersion
- Morning: Explore the Tokyo National Museum.
- Afternoon: Walk through Ueno Park.
- Evening: Enjoy sushi at Tsukiji Outer Market.

Day 3: Modern Tokyo
- Morning: Visit the iconic Shibuya Crossing.
- Afternoon: Shop in Harajuku and Omotesando.
- Evening: Dinner in Shinjuku, explore the nightlife.

Day 4: Day Trip
- Option 1: Day trip to Nikko.
- Option 2: Visit Mount Fuji and the surrounding area.

Day 5: Relaxation and Departure
- Morning: Relax in a traditional onsen (hot spring).
- Afternoon: Last-minute shopping in Ginza.
- Evening: Depart from Tokyo.
